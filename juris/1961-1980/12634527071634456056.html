158 Phil. 442

EN BANC

G.R. No. L-30380, October 21, 1974

LEONARDO GALEON, PETITIONER, VS. MARCIAL GALEON, ZOSIMA GALEON-CANDA, MATEO GALEON, AND COURT OF FIRST INSTANCE OF QUEZON, BRANCH II, LUCENA CITY, RESPONDENTS.

R E S O L U T I O N


ANTONIO, J.:

The Court, after deliberating on the motion of private respondents, RESOLVED:  (a) to deny the reconsideration of the decision of February 28, 1973; and (b) to absolve, for equitable reasons, private respondents from the payment of costs by deleting from the dispositive part of said judgment, the phrase "Costs against private respondents."

Makalintal, C. J., Castro, Fernando, Makasiar, Esguerra, Fernandez, Munoz Palma and Aquino, JJ., concur.

Teehankee, J., maintains his original dissenting vote and concurs in the deletion of costs.

Barredo, J., maintains his original vote.
