Adm. Case No. 512

[ Adm. Case No. 512, July 07, 1962

ESTEBAN DEGAMO, COMPLAINANT VS. TRANQUILINO O. CALO, JR., RESPONDENT.

D E C I S I O N




SUMMARY

Respondent was administratively charged but the charges against him are directly involved in two cases pending with the Court of First Instance of Agusan, namely: Civil Case No. 117 (Tranquilino O. Calo, Jr. vs. Villamor Dizon, et al.) and Criminal Case No. 2347 (People vs. Tranquilino Calo, Jr.). In view of which the investigation of the said charges is held in abeyance until such time that this Court receives notice of the final determination of the two cases mentioned.
