G.R. No. L-15858

G.R. No. L-15858, August 30, 1962

DY LAM GO, PETITIONER-APPELLANT VS. REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES, OPPONENT-APPELLEE.

D E C I S I O N




SUMMARY

A motion for reconsideration was filed by applicant, Dy Lam Go, of the denial of his application for Filipino citizenship on the finding that he failed to prove that he had conducted himself in a proper and irreproachable manner. On passing over the whole record, the Court dismissed the motion on the finding that he lacked lucrative income.

RULING

Petitioner's average income of 3,000.00 as salesman, with n wife and four (4) children to support, is not lucrative income within the moaning of Sec. 2, par. 4 of Com. Act No. 473.
