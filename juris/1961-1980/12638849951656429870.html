VIRGILIO ZAFRA, PETITIONER, VS. WORKMEN'S COMPENSATION COMMISSION AND MANILA PAPER MILLS, INC., RESPONDENTS.

R E S O L U T I O N


GUERRERO, J.:

By reason of a clerical mistake in paragraph (D) of the dispositive portion of the decision on page 6, the words "SIXTY-THREE (P63.00) PESOS" should read "FIFTY-THREE (P53.00) PESOS" as the correct administrative fee.

WHEREFORE, paragraph (D) of the dispositive portion is hereby corrected to read as follows:

(D) To pay FIFTY-THREE (P53.00) PESOS as administrative fee pursuant to Section 55 of the Act.

SO ORDERED.

Teehankee, (Chairman), Santos, and Fernandez, JJ., concur.

Makasiar, J., concurs in a separate opinion.
