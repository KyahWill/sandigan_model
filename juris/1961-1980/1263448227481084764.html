SARBRO & COMPANY, INC., PLAINTIFF-APPELLEE, VS. BRIGIDA VILLANUEVA VDA. DE GARCHITORENA, AND THE REGIS­TER OF DEEDS OF THE PROVINCE OF COTABATO, DEFENDANTS, BRIGIDA VILLANUEVA VDA. DE GARCHITO­RENA, DECEASED, NOW SUBSTITUTED BY DR. SENEN L. R. ASUAN IN HIS CA­PACITY AS ADMINISTRATOR OF HER ESTATE, DEFENDANT-APPELLANT.

D E C I S I O N


ESGUERRA, J.:

This case was certified for decision by the Court of Appeals through the latter's resolution of January 12, 1971, for the reason that only questions of law are involved. It was accepted by this Court through its resolution of Feb­ruary 8, 1971, and docketed as G. R. No. L-33141. There­upon the case was submitted for decision.

On June 29, 1973, both parties through their respec­tive counsel, San Juan, Africa, Gonzales & San Agustin, for the plaintiff-appellee, and Baizas, Alberto & Associates, for the defendant-appellant, Brigida Villanueva Vda. de Gar­chitorena, deceased, substituted by Dr. Senen L. R. Asuan, Administrator of her Estate, submitted a "JOINT MOTION FOR APPROVAL OF COMPROMISE AGREEMENT", dated June 28, 1973, with the compromise agreement attached there­to as Annex "A", which motion with the compromise agreement reads as follows:

"JOINT MOTION FOR APPROVAL
OF COMPROMISE AGREEMENT
"COME NOW the plaintiff-appellee and defendant-appellant, Senen L. R. Asuan, in his capacity as administrator of the Estate of Brigida Villanueva Vda. de Garchitorena, through their respective undersigned counsel, and to this Honor­able Court most respectfully state:

"1.
That on August 21, 1972, they have signed and executed a Compromise Agreement which finally settles the con­troversy subject matter of the above-entitled case; subject, however, to the approval of the agreement by the Court of First Instance which has jurisdiction over the Estate of the late Brigida Villa­nueva Vda. de Garchitorena under Spe­cial Proceedings No. 76765; copy of the said Compromise Agreement is hereto attached as Annex 'A' and made an in­tegral part hereof;
 
"2.
That the Court of First Instance of Manila, in Special Proceedings No. 76765 has approved the Compromise Agreement (Annex 'A' hereof) in an Order dated June 20, 1973, certified true copy of which is hereto attached as Annex 'B' and made an integral part hereof;
 
"3.

That as a consequence of the Compromise Agreement wherein the Estate of Brigida Villanueva Vda. de Garchitorena has relinquished and aban­doned its claim over the parcel of land covered by TCT No. 5214 in favor of herein plaintiff-appellee, there is no more obstacle to the registration of the Deed of Sale with Mortgage, Exhibit 'F'.


"WHEREFORE, it is most re­spectfully prayed that judgment be rendered in accordance with the terms of the Compromise Agree­ment (Annex 'A' hereof) and, fur­ther, that the Register of Deeds of Cotabato be ordered to accept and give due course to the registration of the Deed of Sale with Mortgage, Exhibit 'F'.

"The parties further pray for such other relief which this Honor­able Court may deem just and equit­able under the premises.

Judge "

Finding the said compromise agreement in order and not contrary to law, the same is hereby approved and both parties are enjoined to strictly comply with the terms and conditions thereof.

Pursuant to said motion, the Register of Deeds of the Province of Cotabato shall accept and give due course to the registration of the "Deed of Absolute Sale with Mortgage" (Exhibit "F" in Civil Case No. 1849 of the Court of First In­stance of Cotabato), executed by the deceased, Mariano Gar­chitorena, on September 16, 1961, in favor of plaintiff-appellee, Sarbro & Co., Inc., relative to the land covered by Transfer Certificate of Title No. T-5214.

WHEREFORE, the appeal of the deceased, Brigida Vil­lanueva Vda. de Garchitorena, substituted by the Administrator of the Estate, Dr. Senen L. R. Asuan, and that of the Register of Deeds of the Province of Cotabato, are dismissed and the Register of Deeds of the Province of Cotabato is hereby direct­ed to register the said Deed of Absolute Sale with Mortgage, Exhibit "F". No costs.

Makalintal, (Acting C.J.,) Zaldivar, Ruiz Castro, Teehankee, Fernando, Barredo, Makasiar, and Antonio, JJ., concur.
