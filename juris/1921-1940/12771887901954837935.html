By virtue thereof it is hereby adjudged and decreed that the order of the Court of the First Instance of ___________Bulacan________, dated the __________10th__________ day of __________July____________, nineteen hundred and ___________thirty-three____________, and from which the above-entitled appeal was taken, be, and the same is hereby, affirmed as to the penalty imposed on the appellant, with costs against him in both instances; however said judgment is reversed in so far as it sentences the appellant to indemnify Teodoro Toribio, who is not a party in the case, in the amount of P10.00.
