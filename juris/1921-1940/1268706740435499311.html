CIPRIANA ALVAREZ, ET AL., PLAINTIFFS AND APPELLEES, VS. GETULIO ALMARES ET AL., DEFENDANTS AND APPELLANTS.

D E C I S I O N


MALCOLM, J.:

The defendants appeal from the following judgment rendered by Judge Paras sitting in the Court of First Instance of Ilocos Sur:
POR TODO LO EXPUESTO, este Juzgado dicta decision a favor de los demandantes y en contra de los demandados, y declara por la presente que los terrenos desoritos en la demanda y en la contrademanda son de la propiedad de los demandantes,  con derecho a la posesidn y dominio de los mismos, a los productos que estan podef del depositario,  y ordene a dicho depositario y a los demandados que entreguen dichos terrenos con sus productos a los demandantes.  Con las costas a cargo  de los demandados.

Se absuelve a los demandantes de la contra-demanda y reconvencion.
On examination of the errors assigned and of the record, it will be found that exclusively questions of fact are presented for decision.  We begin with the findings of fact made "by the trial Judge which have  satisfactory evidence supporting them.  There is extant  in the record  both documentary and testimonial proofs which establish the case for the plaintiffs.  We incline to the view that the old documents sufficiently identify the land in question.   As near as we can gather,  the appellees have teen religiously paying the land taxes,   no legal error is noted.

Judgment affirmed, with the coats of this instance against the appellants.

Ten days from the publication of this decision, judgment will tie entered, and five  days thereafter, the record will be remanded to the court below.
