G.R. No. 34485, November 02, 1931

DE BORJA VS. DE BORJA AND DE BORJA

D E C I S I O N




Ownership and possession of land; annulment of conveyance denied. Clear and convincing proof  necessary for annulment. (Arroyo vs. Granada and Gentero, 18 Phil., 484; articles 1300, 1265 and 1269, Civil Code); judgment for defendants affirmed.
