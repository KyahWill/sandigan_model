By virtue thereof it is hereby adjudged and decreed that the order of the Court of the First Instance of __________Bohol_________, dated the _________15th__________ day of __________February_________, nineteen hundred and ___________thirty-four____________, and from which the above-entitled appeal was taken, be, and the same is hereby, affirmed with the modification of reducing the penalty of imprisonment imposed therein, to eight years and one day of prision mayor, it being understood that the minimum of said penalty is 3 years of prision correccional, in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 4103, with the costs in this instance against the appellant.
