SALUSTIANO SUPLADA ET EL., PLAINTIFFS AND APPELLEES, VS. CANDIDO SEGADOR ET AL., DEFENDANTS AND APPELLANTS.

D E C I S I O N


J. STEVENS-ACTING CLERK OF COURT

The Court Having Regularly Acquired Jurisdiction Of The Above-Entitled Cause Submitted By Both Parties For Decision, After Consideration Thereof By The Court Upon The Record, Its Decision End Order For Judgnent Having Been Filed On The 30th Day Of June, A. D. Nineteen Hundred And Thirty-Four;

By virtue thereof it is hereby adjudged and decreed that the appeal is dismissed with costs in both instances against the defendants-appellants.

It is further ordered that the plaintiffs-appellees recover from the defendants-appellants the sum of P40.00 as costs.
