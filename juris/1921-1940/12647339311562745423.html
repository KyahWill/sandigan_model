The defense of alibi is always received with cautionc (People v» Badilia, 48 Phil, 718o) It should be proved by probable evidence which reasonably satisfies the court of the truth of such defense. As the crime of robbery with rape is specially define in paragraph 2 of article 294 of the Revised Penal Code, not as two distinct, offenses but as an indivisible complex crime, and penalized with a single penalty, there is consequently no force in the argument that.the present prosecution should have been commenced upon a complaint signed and filed by the offended party, Julia Lacsamanaj victim of the rape.

The offense committed by the appellants falls under paragraph 2 of article 294 of the Revised Penal Code which fixes, the penalty of reclusion temporal in its medium period to reclusion perpetua There being present in this case'the aggravating circumstances of nocturnity and that the crime was committed by a band and in the dwelling of the offended parties, without arty mitigating circumstance, the appropriate penalty is reclusion perpetua which is hereby imposed upon the appellants.

As thus modified, the appealed judgment is affirmed, wiiii costs of both Instances proportionately against the appellants. So ordered.
