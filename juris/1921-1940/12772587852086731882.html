PHILIPPINE RAILWAYCOMPANY, PETITIONER-APPELLANT, PETITIONER-APPELLANT, VS. CEBU AUTOBUS COMPANY, FILIPINO BUS TRANSPORTATION CO., FRANCISCO LOZADA, CARMELINO CABRILLO, FELIX GANTUANGCO, ANGEL MONTEMAYOR AND VIDAL JIMENO, RESPONDENTS-APPELLEES.

D E C I S I O N


J. STEVENS-ACTING CLERK OF COURT

The Court having regularly acquired jurisdiction of the above-entitled causa submitted by both parties for decision, after consideration thereof by the Court upon the record, its decision and order for judgment having been filed on the 11th day of May, nineteen hundred and thirtyfour;

By virtue thereof it is hereby adjudged end decreed that the decision of the public Service Commission dated the 8th day of August, nineteen hundred and thirty-three, and from which the above-entitled appeal was taken, be, and the same is hereby affirmed in all its parts, without special pronouncement as to costs
