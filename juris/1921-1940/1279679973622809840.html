ERLANGER & GALINGER, INC., PLAINTIFF AND APPELLANT, VS. ISAAC GUTIEREZ, DEFENDANT AND APPELLEE.

D E C I S I O N


J. STEVENS-ACTING CLERK OF COURT

This court having regularly acquired jurisdiction for the trial of the above-entitled cause submitted by both parties for decision,after consideration thereof by the Court upon the record, its decision and order for judgment having been filed on the 28th day of July, A. D. nineteen hundred and thirty-four;

By virtue thereof it is hereby adjudged and decreed that so much of the judgment appealed from as dismissed the complaint of the plaintiff is affirmed, and so much of the judgment as allowed the counterclaim of the defendant is reversed, with the result that neither party recover anything from the other, without special pronouncement as to costs in either instance.
