G.R. No. L-4585

G.R. No. L-4585, October 24, 1952

THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE, VS. TORIBIO ALVAR, DEFENDANT AND APPELLANT.

D E C I S I O N




Treason. Appeal from a decision of the Court of First Instance of Davao sentencing the appellant to suffer reclusion perpetua and to pay a fine of P10,000 and to indemnify the heirs of each deceased in the sum of P6,000 and to pay the costs.

Judgment affirmed with costs.
