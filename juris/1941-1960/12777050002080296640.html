G.R. Nos. L-4673 & L-4675

G.R. Nos. L-4673 & L-4675, January 25, 1952

TRANQUILINO CALO, ET AL., VS. LUIS ORTEGA, AS JUDGE OF THE COURT OF FIRST INSTANCE OF LAGUNA, REGINO RELOVA, TEODULA BARTOLOME, JULIANA SALASIBAR, TRINIDAD DE LEON-ARENAS, AND JUSTO ARENAS, RESPONDENTS.

D E C I S I O N




Petition for certiorari with preliminary injunction, to enjoin defendants from tilling the lands and working or harvesting the standing crops thereon, of which plaintiffs claim to be the owners of and actual possessors.

Petition denied with costs against the petitioners. The writ is dissolved.
