MARCOS LORENZO VS. DIRECTOR OF PRISONS.

D E C I S I O N




MARCOS LORENZO VS. DIRECTOR OF PRISONS.
The Solicitor General manifested that finding the allegations in the petition for a writ of habeas corpus well founded, the respondent has no objection to the release of prisoner named in said petition. So, the petition is granted and respondent is ordered to immediately release the prisoner.
