D E C I S I O N




ELENA GONZALES VS. JUDGE BONIFACIO YSIP AND ADELA VELASQUEZ.
For the reasons stated in Gonzales vs. Ysip et al., L-919, supra, p. 662, the order of the respondent Judge rejecting certain documents that had been presented for purposes of reconstitution, is set aside; he is ordered to admit them and to declare the case to which the documents relate, as duly reconstituted.
