D E C I S I O N




ESTER LEONIO ET AL., PETITIONERS, VS. HON. EMILIO PEÑA, ETC., ET AL., RESPONDENTS.
JUDGMENTS; EXECUTION THEREOF; MARKET STALLS.—Petition to annul the order of the respondent Judge authorizing the issuance of a writ of execution in civil case No. 952 of the Court of First Instance of Manila (Ester Leonio et al. vs. Valeriano Fugoso et al.). Buenaventura vs. Peña et al. (78 Phil. 795), a similar case, about market stalls, and in connection with "execution" of judgment, is held applicable to this case.
