G.R. No. L-4396, April 25, 1952

THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLANT VS. JESUS MATUGAS AND ANTONIO GICA, DEFENDANTS AND APPELLEES.

D E C I S I O N
