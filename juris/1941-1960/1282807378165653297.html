CANDIDA L. DE LICAUCO ET AL. VS. ADELINA REYES ESTANIEL ET AL.
When there is no agreed term for the lease and the rent is paid monthly, the lease is deemed from month to month; and it ends upon the expiration of the month without necessity of special notice (art. 1581, C. C.; Estrella vs. Sangalang, L-65, February 6, 1945; Buhay vs. Cobarrubias, L-43, February 27, 1946; Co Tiamco vs. Diaz, etc., et al., 42 Off. Gaz., 1169).
