G.R. No. L-4411

G.R. No. L-4411, February 08, 1952

THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE VS. MARIANO CABEDA AND LUDOVICO CABEDA, DEFENDANTS AND APPELLANTS.

D E C I S I O N




Murder. Judgment of the lower court modified by imposing upon Mariano Cabeda for the crime of murder the penalty of reclusion perpetua and upon Ludovico Cabeda for homicide, the penalty from eight (8) years, eight (8) months and one (1) day of prision mayor to fourteen (14) years, eight (8) months, and one (1) day of reclusion temporal, with the accessory penalties of the law. In all other respects the decision is affirmed with costs against the appellants.
