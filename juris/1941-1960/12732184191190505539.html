G.R. No. L-13024

G.R. No. L-13024, September 30, 1959

PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE, VS. MAXIMO CA-ANDI AND FELICISIMO COBAOBAO, DEFENDANTS AND APPELLANTS.

D E C I S I O N


PER CURIAM:

Review of a judgment of the Court of First of Agusan finding defendants guilty of robbery with multiple homicide and sentencing them each to suffer the penalty of death with the accessory penalties, to jointly and severally indemnity the heirs of the deceased Leona Agustin, Arcadia Domingo, Herico Domingo, and Carlito Domecil in the sum of P6,000 and each to pay one-third of the  costs.
