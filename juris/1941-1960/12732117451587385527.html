G.R. No. L-11072

G.R. No. L-11072, September 24, 1959

ANACLETO MAURICIO, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLANT, VS. MAURA VILLANUEVA, BEATRIZ IÑIGO, AMELIA IÑIGO, AND, CANDIDA AGUSTIN, DEFENDANTS AND APPELLEES.

D E C I S I O N


CONCEPCION, J.:

Appeal by plaintiff from an order of the Court of First Instance of Isabela granting a motion to dismiss of defendants Maura Villanueva and Beatriz, Benjamin, Federico, Amelia, and Leonida, all surnamed Iñigo, on the ground of prescription of action, and dismissing the amended complaint, insofar as defendants are concerned, without prejudice to plaintiff's right to file a separate action against defendant Candida Agustin if he so desires.
