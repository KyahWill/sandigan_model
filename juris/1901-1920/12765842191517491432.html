D E C I S I O N

The facts in this case are exactly analogous to the facts in the case Murciano vs. Valenton, Vol 3. Phil. Rep. 537.  Applying the doctrine established in that case to the facts in the present, we are of the opinion and so hold that the judgment of the lower court should be affirmed, with costs, and it is so ordered.
