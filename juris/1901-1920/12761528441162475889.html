G.R. No. 3978

G.R. No. 3978, February 18, 1908

UNITED STATES, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE, VS. PIO BAÑEZ AND RUFINO CORDOSO, DEFENDANTS AND APPELLANTS.

D E C I S I O N


CARSON, J.:

ATTEMPT AGAINST AUTHORITIES.—From the Court of First Instance of Isabela. Evidence held sufficient to show the guilt of the accused beyond a reasonable doubt.  No error prejudicial to the rights of the accused appearing, judgment affirmed.

Per Carson, J.

For appellants: Alberto Barretto.
For appellee: Attorney-General Araneta.
