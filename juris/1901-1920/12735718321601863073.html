G.R. No.5475

G.R. No.5475, December 16, 1909

THE UNITED STATES, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE, VS. ANTONIO DE LAS ARMAS, DEFENDANT AND APPELLANT.

D E C I S I O N




Defendant was convicted of abusos deshonestos and sentenced to three years six months ans twenty-one days of prision correccional with accessories.  No reason appearing why the findings should be disturbed, judgment affirmed.

Per Moreland, J.
