G.R. No. 5298

G.R. No. 5298, December 17, 1909

THE UNITED STATES, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE, VS. TIBURCIO VILLANUEVA, DEFENDANT AND APPELLANT.

D E C I S I O N


SMITH, J.:

Defendant was convicted of illegal marriage and sentenced to nine years of prision mayor,  Evidence justifies the findings and no prejudicial error appears.  Defense raised a question as to the construction of articles 440 and 471 of the Penal Code, but this was adversely decided in U.S. vs. San Jose (page 713, supra).  Judgment affirmed.


Per Carson, J.
