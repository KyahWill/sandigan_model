G.R. No. 4920

G.R. No. 4920, October 04, 1909

JUAN TOLENTINO AND MODESTA TOLENTINO, PLAINTIFFS AND APPELLANTS, VS. PEDRO RAMIREZ, AS ADMINISTRATOR OF THE PROPERTY OF VICTORINO BUHAY, AND CECILIO ROSAL, AS SHERIFF OF THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, DEFENDANTS AND APPELLEES.

D E C I S I O N


MORELAND, J.:

ACTION TO RECOVER REALTY; OWNERSHIP. — From the Court of First Instance of Batangas. Powell, J.

Action by the plaintiffs, children of Eugenio Tolentino and Maria A. IIustre, to recover land alleged to have belonged to their father.  The evidence shows that it was purchased by defendant Ramirez at a judicial sale which arose out an action by Ramirez against Tolentino.  Judgment in favor of the defendants affirmed.
