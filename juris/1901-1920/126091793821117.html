It will be seen that the witness Feliciano Maglaqui, instead of writing the name of the testatrix on the will, wrote his own. Probate of the will was refused in the court below on the ground that the name of the testatrix was not signed thereto, and the petitioner has appealed. The question presented has been decided adversely to the appellant in the following cases: Ex parte Pedro Arcenas et al.,[1] No. 1708, August 24, 1905 (4 Off. Gaz., 568); Ex parte Nemesio Belfin Santiago,[2] No. 2002, August 18, 1905 (4 Off. Gaz., 507.)

The judgment of the court below is affirmed, with the costs of this instance against the appellant, and after the expiration of twenty days judgment should be entered in accordance herewith and the case remanded to the court below for execution. So ordered.

Arellano, C. J., Mapa, Johnson, and Carson, JJ., concur.
