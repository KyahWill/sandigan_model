G.R. No. 2035

G.R. No. 2035, December 24, 1909

THE UNITED STATES, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE, VS. DEAN TOMPKINS, DEFENDANT AND APPELLANT.

D E C I S I O N




Defendant was convicted of misappropriation of public funds, under article 390, Penal Code, and sentenced to nine years of presidio mayor, to pay an indemnity, and to disqualification from public office.  Judgment affirmed.

Per  Carson, J.
