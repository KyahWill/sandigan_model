5 Phil. 49

G.R. No. 2879, September 25, 1905

EDWIN CASE, PLAINTIFF AND APPELLEE, VS. THE METROPOLE HOTEL AND RESTAURANT, SOCIEDAD ANONIMA, DEFENDANT AND APPELLANT.

D E C I S I O N


CARSON, J.:

This case having come here on appeal, the defendant and appellant moves for a stay of execution of the judgment pending the appeal.

It appears that the trial court denied a similar motion, and we are of opinion that no change has taken place in the status of the parties or of the subject-matter, which would justify our interference to modify or annul the effect of the ruling of the court below.

Arellano, C. J., Torres, Mapa, Johnson, and Willard, JJ., concur.
