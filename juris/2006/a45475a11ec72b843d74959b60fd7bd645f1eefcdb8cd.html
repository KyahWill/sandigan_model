A.M. No. 06-2-09-SC

EN BANC

A.M. NO. 06-2-09-SC, February 21, 2006

RE: CREATION OF A COMMITTEE TO REVISE THE BENCHBOOK ON PENALTIES


SIRS/MESDAMES:

Quoted hereunder, for your information, is a resolution of this Court dated FEB 21 2006.

"A.M. No. 06-2-09-SC.- Re: Creation of a Committee to Revise the Benchbook on Penalties.- The Court Resolved, upon the recommendation of Associate Justice Romeo J. Callejo, Sr., to CREATE the Committee to Revise the Benchbook on Penalties, to be chaired by Justice Edilberto G. Sandoval, with Justices Lucas Bersamin of the Court of Appeals and Diosdado Peralta of the Sandiganbayan and RTC Judge Mario Lopez as Members thereof."

Very truly yours,

(SGD.) MA. LUISA D. VILLARAMA
Clerk of Court
